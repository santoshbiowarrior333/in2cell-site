◐ Name & logo concept

One mark for water & cycle.

The idea: tides and your period are both rhythms ruled by the moon. So the logo is a single icon — a water droplet whose inside is a crescent moon. Water + cycle, fused into one symbol.

My recommendation

Lunara.
your rhythm, in flow
Luna (moon → your cycle & the tides) + aqua / -ara (water). A coined, ownable name — soft, feminine and modern, easy to say in any language.

How the mark works

Droplet = water

The hydration / drinking-water side of the app.

Crescent = cycle

The moon — your period & its monthly rhythm.

Together = Lunara

One symbol carries both — that’s the innovation.
