Other Sanskrit options

Sudhaसुधा
The moon’s nectar — the ambrosia (amrita) that drips from the moon. Soft, feminine, luminous; pure “liquid of the moon.”
Jalenduजलेन्दु
jala (water) + indu (moon) → “water-moon”: the moon mirrored in water. A coined, poetic compound.
Chandrikaचन्द्रिका
Moonlight. Gentle and cooling — in Ayurveda the moon governs water & calm. Graceful and widely loved.
Saumyaसौम्य
“Of the moon (Soma) — gentle, cool, serene.” Soma is both the moon and a sacred fluid. Calm & nurturing.
